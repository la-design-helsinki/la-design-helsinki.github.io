The Pandemic 2020

Digital illustrations
Love is in the middle of chaos.
While searching for news of Italy coping with the Corvid-19 disaster, I encountered some nice images that depict how the Italian citizens reacted to the lockdown order. Even though people stay at home, they cheered up each other in many creative ways. Some played music, some sang along, some danced, and some started thinking about how they should take care of their beloved ones.

By creating the paintings of these people, I would love to share with you positive energy, whether you have been under a lockdown or fighting to overcome the crises that occur during this tough time.

Stay safe and strong!
